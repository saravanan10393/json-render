## Overview
Warm, inviting storefront energy for e-commerce, food, and retail apps — cream surfaces, terracotta calls-to-action, generous rounding.

## Colors
Cream canvas with deep warm ink. Terracotta primary draws the eye to add-to-cart moments; amber-tinted accents for hovers and highlights.

## Typography
Headings use Fraunces; body text uses Inter. Keep heading weights confident and body comfortable for long reading.
