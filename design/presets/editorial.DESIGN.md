## Overview
Paper-and-ink editorial feel for blogs, publications, and content tools — serif display headings, restrained palette, crimson accents.

## Colors
Near-black ink as the primary itself (buttons read like print), warm paper neutrals, crimson reserved for emphasis and destructive moments.

## Typography
Headings use Playfair Display; body text uses Source Sans 3. Keep heading weights confident and body comfortable for long reading.
